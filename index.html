About

Club Swimming is a co-ed team with about 120 active members. We are part of College Club Swimming (CCS)
which is the collective organization that unifies college club swim programs across the country.

We regularly hold practices and compete in meets in both the fall and spring semesters. There are three
meets per semester, two of those being "day" meets where we travel to schools within Virginia and one
overnight/weekend meet.

We attend CCS Nationals in the spring semester every year.

How to Join

We do not hold tryouts nor do we make cuts.

We encourage anyone with or without swimming experience to attend our New Member Interest Meeting which
takes place soon after Student Organization Night in September. After this meeting, we will hold a week of
practices in which new members will practice together and get to meet some people before introducing them
into practicing with returning members.

JMU Club Swim Email List

This is the best way to join / receive updates on the team.

In order to subscribe to the JMU Club Swim email list, please send an email to jmuclubswimming@gmail.com
Please make the subject "Email List" and provide your full name, phone number, and email address.

If you are interested in joining please state your interest in your email and one of our exec members will
respond to you shortly with more information.

In order to unsubscribe from the email list, please send an email with the subject "Unsubscribe" and list
your name in the body.

Dues

Dues are $85 per semester or $150 for the full year
This covers everything from weekend social events to travel expenses for meets and Nationals.
